    How We Started

    John qualified as a chef during a 4 year hotel management course at the Birmingham College of Food & Domestic Arts, (now Birmingham University). After 12 years of employment with large hotel groups he became self employed along with Janet in 1973. We had our own country hotel and restaurant in the UK until we moved here to Cala Bona in 1987 and opened this restaurant.

    John retired in 2007 and Janet in 2019. Son Lorne (aka John) along with wife Yoli are now at the helm. Lorne having grown up in the restaurant and worked front of house since 1998.

    Our Priority

    At Janet & Johns, it is always our top priority to provide our guests the best possible care in our restaurant, as well as to ensure the health and safety of our guests, staff and our community.
